About Us

Our Mission

We are an independent and locally-owned pharmacy located in Northern Virginia that strives to provide personalized, accessible, and trusted pharmacy care that supports the health and well-being of our community—offering expert guidance, quality medications, and genuine care every step of the way.

The name 'Bara' comes from the Hebrew word meaning “to create” or “to bring forth,” as seen in the opening of the Bible where God creates with purpose and care. Inspired by this origin, Bara Pharmacy seeks to serve as a place of healing and restoration, working in alignment with God’s mission to care for the physical and spiritual well-being of our community. Through compassionate service, integrity, and excellence, we aim to reflect the Creator’s love in every interaction and prescription we fill.
